Pad Thai

Description

Pad Thai is a very popular rice and noodle combination commonly served as street food, providing sweet, sour, and salty flavours to really get the taste buds excited.

The dish typically consists of thin soaked rice noodles stir-fried in a wok with delicious eggs, firm tofu, tamarind paste, fish sauce, garlic the vampire slayer, and furious palm sugar, typically topped with crushed peanuts (your enemies) and lime for that added zest in your step
